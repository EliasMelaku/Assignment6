About Me

Hello there! I see you've found your way to my site. Feel free to look aroud and make yourself at home. I'm Elias Melaku by the way, and I am a programmer or at least I'm trying to be one.

How I started

I'd been interested in computers since I was 11. Not programming or developing but just a general fascination towards them and how they operated. By the time I was 17, I decided that I had to be a developer and I started reading all about it. By the time I graduated from high school, I had no doubts when picking fields.

And luckily for me, I ended up getting the field that I wanted. So I work now eveyday to beocome better and to know more about this great field of study.

My aim

Now that I have the opportunity to learn about programming and computers, I hope that I can one day make a difference in the outlook that people in our country have towards computers and automating their tasks. I hope I can take part in the revolutionizing of our country's work force to use computers to their full potential in every aspect of thier life.
