My Education

In order for me to acheive the goals Ive set for myself, I saw it fit to take the education that was necessary. And so I'm currently taking my courses at AAiT , the Faculty of Technology and a branch of Addis Ababa University. If you are interested in taking a course in either, follow the links and check the addmissions process.

And luckily for me, I ended up getting the field that I wanted. So I work now eveyday to beocome better and to know more about this great field of study.

Should we go?

Some people might think its old fashioned, and that going to universities is now becoming obsolete, but I still think that it is good to attend these types of education centers. Some things are found in the universities, that can't be found elsewhere or may take much more time to figure out if we tried them ourseleves.

But, we do need aid from other sources and thankfully, for my field there are tons of resources on the internet and I am only a click away from getting those. I you are also interested in programming and related fields, I'll leave you with a great way to get started by showing you some of the materials I used and still use to this day.
